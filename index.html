ABOUT

More than meets the eye

Established in June 1994, Horizons Technology is a leading I.T and software consultancy firm in Melbourne, Australia, serving national and multi-national clients across diverse sectors while maintaining a competitive edge through innovation and best practices.

Company Inception

In June of 1994, Horizons Technology was incorporated with a vision to offer top-tier I.T and software consultancy services to a diverse clientele, including both national and multi-national companies in Melbourne, Australia.

Strategic Partnership

Just six months after its inception, Vinux Pty Ltd forged a strategic alliance with Horizons Technology, infusing invaluable expertise and effectively augmenting our software engineering capabilities.

Expansive Reach

Over the years, Horizons Technology has significantly expanded its reach and portfolio. To date, we have successfully delivered consultancy and software support services to a wide spectrum of clients, encompassing various sectors such as Banking & Finance, Logistics, Telecommunications, Transport, Education, and Government.

Staying Competitive

Our commitment to staying at the forefront of the industry is unwavering. We continuously embrace the latest software technology and adhere to best practices, ensuring our ability to remain competitive in the fast-paced and ever-evolving world of technology and consultancy.

Client-Centric Approach

Horizons Technology's success is underpinned by its unwavering commitment to delivering client-centric I.T and software consultancy services, fostering strong relationships with clients across diverse sectors in Melbourne, Australia, and beyond.
